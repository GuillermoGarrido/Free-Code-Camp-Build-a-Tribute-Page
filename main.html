Bernie Sanders

Our Revolution: A Future to Believe in

Bernie Sanders was born on September 8, 1941, in Brooklyn, New York. Moreover, he is one of Vermont's current Senators. He notably ran for President of the United States in 2016 as a member of the Democratic Party, before controversially losing to Democratic Party presidential primary to Hillary Clinton. He is considered by many to be one of the U.S. government's true progressives. Although he failed to win the U.S. Presidential Election, his movement has inspired millions of Americans to join the progressive revolution he began.

    Some Fun Facts About Bernie Sanders:
  • Bernie Sanders graduated from the University of Chicago with B.A. in 1964.
  • Bernie Sanders served a whopping total of 8 consectutive terms in the U.S. House of Representatives from 1991 to 2007.
  • Bernie Sanders currently holds the record for the longest serving Independent politician to hold a seat in the U.S. House of Representatives.
  • He also served as the Mayor of Burlington, Vermont, from 1981 to 1989.
  • In 1963, Bernie Sanders joined a cohort of students who rode buses from Chicago to Washington D.C. in order to participate in the March on Washington for Jobs and Freedom.
  • He recently published a book titled Our Revolution, in which he discusses his 2016 Presidential Campaign as well as his stances on various political issues
